    Cordoba, Spain

    Jazz bassist, cellist, singer, and songwriter

    She released the acclaimed albums Junjo, Esperanza and Chamber Music Society before winning a Grammy in 2011 for Best New Artist, creating history. She released the more pop/soul-driven Radio Music Society in 2012, and has performed at the Oscars.
